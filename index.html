Rent Vs Buy Calculator

A buy vs rent calculator is a tool used to assess the financial implications of buying or renting a house. It helps individuals make an informed decision by comparing the costs associated with purchasing a home versus renting one. The calculator takes into account various factors, such as the purchase price, down payment, interest rates, monthly mortgage payments, rental costs, and other expenses.
